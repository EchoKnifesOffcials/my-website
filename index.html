Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I earn credits?
Exchange your MM2 items in the Exchange tab! Each item gives credits minus a small 20 credit fee.
❓ Is cross-trading allowed?
NO! Cross trading (MM2 for non-MM2 items) is strictly prohibited and results in a permanent ban.
❓ How do I verify my Roblox account?
Go to Profile → Verify Roblox, enter your username, and click verify. Your avatar and username will be displayed on trades.
❓ How do I create a trade?
Go to the Trades page and click "Create Trade". Select items you offer and want, then publish for everyone to see!
